## Ikhtisar \u2014 Bahasa Indonesia

Air Terjun Tegenungan adalah salah satu air terjun paling populer dan mudah diakses di Bali. Berbeda dengan air terjun lain yang umumnya terletak di pegunungan utara, Tegenungan berada di dataran rendah Gianyar, sehingga sangat dekat dengan Ubud maupun area selatan Bali.

Aliran airnya cukup deras, terutama selama musim hujan. Kawasan ini telah dikelola dengan baik dan menyediakan berbagai fasilitas, termasuk restoran, loker, dan banyak titik foto buatan. Pengunjung harus menuruni sekitar 168 anak tangga yang tertata rapi untuk mencapai kolam di dasar air terjun. 

Meski kolam di bawahnya terlihat mengundang, pengunjung harus sangat berhati-hati saat berenang, terutama saat arus sedang kuat. Karena popularitasnya, tempat ini cenderung sangat padat setelah jam 10 pagi.

## Overview \u2014 English

Tegenungan Waterfall is one of the most popular and accessible waterfalls in Bali. Unlike most of Bali's waterfalls, which are located in the northern highlands, Tegenungan is situated in the lowlands of Gianyar, making it an easy trip from both Ubud and southern Bali.

The waterfall features a powerful cascade, especially during the rainy season. The area is well-developed with facilities including restaurants, lockers, and numerous constructed photo spots. Visitors must descend approximately 168 paved steps to reach the base of the falls.

While the pool at the bottom is inviting, visitors should exercise caution if swimming, particularly when the current is strong. Due to its accessibility and fame, the site gets very crowded from mid-morning onwards.
