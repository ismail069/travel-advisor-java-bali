## Overview — English

Sekumpul is one of North Bali’s most dramatic waterfall areas. Its appeal comes from multiple streams visible within a green valley rather than a single drop. The upper viewpoints are already impressive, but reaching the lower area requires a descent that must later be climbed again.

The route can include stairs, slopes, wet surfaces, and slippery or crossing sections depending on conditions. Rainfall upstream can change water volume and safety quickly. Visitors should wear footwear with reliable grip, carry water, and follow local instructions.

Official Buleleng and Love Bali sources describe Sekumpul as a collection of several waterfalls. However, ticketing, guide arrangements, open routes, and operating times can change. All operational information should be checked at the official entrance before publication and travel.
