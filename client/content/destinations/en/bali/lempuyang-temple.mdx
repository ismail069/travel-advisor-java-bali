## Overview — English

Pura Penataran Agung Lempuyang, part of the wider Pura Luhur Lempuyang complex, is one of Bali's oldest and most highly regarded temples. Situated high on the slopes of Mount Lempuyang in Karangasem, the temple became a global sensation due to its magnificent split gateway that perfectly frames Mount Agung in the distance—a vista universally known as the "Gate of Heaven."

For most international visitors, capturing a photo at this gateway is the main objective. However, this immense popularity means visitors must be prepared for extreme queuing times, often waiting 2 to 4 hours just for a photo. To manage traffic, private vehicles must park at a terminal down the mountain, and a mandatory shuttle bus transports visitors up to the temple. Beyond the photo spot, the temple is part of a sacred pilgrimage route involving thousands of steps leading to the summit shrines.
