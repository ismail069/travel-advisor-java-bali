## Overview — English

Ubud Art Market is one of the town center’s best-known shopping stops. Products include woven bags, textiles, clothing, woodcraft, paintings, accessories, and decorative goods. Quality and origin vary between stalls, so buyers should inspect materials and workmanship.

Bargaining remains common, but it should be respectful. Opening prices do not always represent the final price, yet travelers should avoid pushing negotiations to a point that dismisses the value of craft labor.

Opening hours vary by source and vendor. Some stalls begin earlier or remain open later. Morning visits reduce crowd pressure and allow more time to compare products.

## Practical Planning / Perencanaan Praktis

Pusat Ubud padat, terutama menjelang siang dan sore. Walking route dapat mengurangi kebutuhan parkir, tetapi trotoar, penyeberangan, panas, dan hujan tetap perlu diperhitungkan. Untuk museum, periksa apakah tiket mencakup minuman, workshop, pertunjukan, atau akses khusus.
