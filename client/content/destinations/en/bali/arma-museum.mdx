## Overview — English

The Agung Rai Museum of Art, or ARMA, is located in Pengosekan and functions as more than a museum. The experience may include galleries, gardens, traditional buildings, performances, workshops, and educational activities.

Its collections create space for Balinese art as well as Indonesian and international work connected to Bali. The compound is larger than several central Ubud museums, so a very short visit may not do justice to its broader program.

ARMA’s official website should be the primary reference for opening hours, admission, performance schedules, workshops, and temporary exhibitions. None of these should be assumed to remain fixed.

The official site does not state one photography rule for every collection and program. Confirm permission for each gallery or event and do not assume admission grants artwork reproduction rights.

## Practical Planning / Perencanaan Praktis

Pusat Ubud padat, terutama menjelang siang dan sore. Walking route dapat mengurangi kebutuhan parkir, tetapi trotoar, penyeberangan, panas, dan hujan tetap perlu diperhitungkan. Untuk museum, periksa apakah tiket mencakup minuman, workshop, pertunjukan, atau akses khusus.
