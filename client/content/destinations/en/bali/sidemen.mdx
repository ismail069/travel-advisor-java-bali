## Overview — English

Sidemen Valley is frequently described as "Ubud from 20 years ago." Tucked away in the foothills of Mount Agung in Karangasem, this rural area offers the classic, untouched Balinese landscapes that many travelers seek. Emerald green rice terraces, deep river valleys, and the towering volcanic peak create an atmosphere of profound tranquility.

The primary activity in Sidemen is slowing down and embracing the pace of village life. Visitors typically spend their days trekking through the rice paddies, visiting traditional weaving workshops renowned for their intricate Songket and Endek textiles, or partaking in white-water rafting on the nearby Telaga Waja River. Thanks to its peaceful environment, Sidemen has become a highly sought-after location for beautiful eco-resorts and boutique bamboo villas that blend seamlessly into the jungle.
