## Overview — English

Museum Puri Lukisan is one of Ubud’s important art institutions. Its collections help visitors understand the development of Balinese painting and woodcarving, including stylistic change, artist–patron relationships, and the role of art organizations.

The museum consists of several gallery buildings set within gardens. Because the collection is substantial, visitors should avoid rushing from room to room. Reading labels and following the exhibition sequence produces a more meaningful experience.

The official website provides location, opening, admission, collection, and historical information. Hours of 09:00–17:00 appear consistently across several sources, while current admission and workshop programs should still be rechecked.

The official visitor page does not state photography rules for every gallery. Do not assume artwork photography is allowed; follow signs and staff. Admission does not transfer copyright in artworks or collection imagery.

## Practical Planning / Perencanaan Praktis

Pusat Ubud padat, terutama menjelang siang dan sore. Walking route dapat mengurangi kebutuhan parkir, tetapi trotoar, penyeberangan, panas, dan hujan tetap perlu diperhitungkan. Untuk museum, periksa apakah tiket mencakup minuman, workshop, pertunjukan, atau akses khusus.
