## Overview — English

Pura Taman Saraswati, also known as the Ubud Water Palace, is a central Ubud temple dedicated to Dewi Saraswati, the Hindu goddess of knowledge, literature, and the arts. Its defining visual feature is the approach to the temple between two lotus ponds.

The location is highly convenient. The temple is close to Ubud Palace, Ubud Art Market, Museum Puri Lukisan, and the restaurants along Jalan Raya Ubud. A short visit fits naturally into a central-town walking route.

The temple remains a sacred place. Visitors must follow dress rules, respect restricted areas, and avoid disturbing worship. Not every section of the complex is open for tourism.

## Best Time

Morning provides softer light, lower heat, and fewer visitors. Late afternoon works well when combining the temple with dinner or an evening performance. Lotus flowering changes with season and plant condition.
