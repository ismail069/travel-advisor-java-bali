## Overview — English

Samasta Lifestyle Village is an open-air lifestyle center in Jimbaran designed for dining, shopping, and relaxed social time within one district. Its official website describes Samasta as “The Hub of Jimbaran” and presents a mix of restaurants, cafés, shops, family entertainment, and lifestyle services.

This is not an attraction that normally requires an entire sightseeing day. Its value becomes stronger when combined with other South Bali activities, including Jimbaran Beach, Garuda Wisnu Kencana, the airport, nearby hotels, or a route toward Uluwatu.

Late afternoon to evening is usually the most useful visiting period, when temperatures are more comfortable and dining becomes the main focus. Travelers visiting one tenant may stay briefly, while families or groups combining food, shopping, and events can spend several hours.

## Why Visit?

- Convenient dining stop in Jimbaran.
- Multiple food choices in one managed area.
- Useful for families and mixed groups.
- Easy to combine with Jimbaran Beach or GWK.
- Suitable during rain or when travelers want a less demanding activity.
- Offers souvenirs, fashion, desserts, entertainment, and casual meeting places.

## Waktu Terbaik

### Siang

Cocok untuk makan siang, kopi, belanja, atau aktivitas indoor. Namun area terbuka dapat terasa panas.

### Sore

Pilihan terbaik untuk datang sebelum makan malam. Wisatawan dapat berjalan, melihat tenant, lalu memilih tempat makan.

### Malam

Suasana paling hidup biasanya terjadi pada malam hari, terutama akhir pekan atau saat acara. Periksa jadwal kegiatan resmi bila ingin menghindari atau mengikuti keramaian.

## Best Time

### Midday

Suitable for lunch, coffee, shopping, or indoor activities. Open-air areas may feel hot.

### Late Afternoon

The most practical arrival period before dinner. Visitors can walk around, compare tenants, and choose a restaurant.

### Evening

The center is usually livelier at night, especially on weekends or event dates. Check official event information when planning around crowds.

## Suggested Visit Duration

- One meal or coffee: 60–90 minutes
- Dining plus shopping: 2–3 hours
- Family visit with entertainment or event: 3–4 hours

## Getting There

Samasta is located on Jalan Wanagiri in Jimbaran, near Mövenpick Resort & Spa Jimbaran Bali.

### From Jimbaran Beach

The center is close enough to combine with a beach visit, though the exact walking or driving route depends on the starting beach section.

### From Ngurah Rai Airport

Samasta is geographically close to the airport, but evening traffic can increase travel time.

### From Nusa Dua

The destination works well as a dining or shopping stop after Nusa Dua Beach, Water Blow, or a conference schedule.

### Taxi and Ride-Hailing

Use the official name “Samasta Lifestyle Village.” Pick-up zones may be adjusted during events.
