## Ikhtisar \u2014 Bahasa Indonesia

Pasar Seni Sukawati (Sukawati Art Market) adalah salah satu pasar seni paling tua dan paling terkenal di Bali, berlokasi di Jalan Raya Sukawati, Gianyar. Pasar ini merupakan surga bagi wisatawan yang ingin membeli suvenir khas Bali seperti pakaian (termasuk daster dan sarung pantai), lukisan, patung kayu, perhiasan perak, dan berbagai kerajinan tangan lokal. 

Berbelanja di Sukawati memberikan pengalaman budaya tersendiri karena pembeli diharapkan untuk melakukan tawar-menawar harga dengan pedagang. Harga awal yang ditawarkan biasanya cukup tinggi, sehingga keterampilan menawar sangat dibutuhkan (sering kali bisa ditawar hingga 30-50% dari harga awal). 

Karena pasar ini bisa menjadi sangat panas dan sesak pada siang hari, waktu terbaik untuk berkunjung adalah pagi hari. Selain udaranya masih segar, penduduk lokal percaya bahwa pembeli pertama di pagi hari (disebut *penglaris*) membawa keberuntungan, sehingga pedagang lebih mudah memberikan harga murah.

## Overview \u2014 English

Sukawati Art Market is one of Bali's oldest and most renowned traditional markets, located on Jalan Raya Sukawati in Gianyar. It is a haven for travelers looking to purchase Balinese souvenirs, including clothing (such as sarongs and resort wear), paintings, wood carvings, silver jewelry, and a myriad of local handicrafts.

Shopping at Sukawati offers a cultural experience in itself, as haggling is expected and part of the daily trading culture. Vendors typically quote a high initial price, so good bargaining skills are essential—it's common to negotiate down to 30-50% of the asking price.

Because the market can get very hot and congested during the day, the best time to visit is early in the morning. Not only is the weather cooler, but local vendors also believe that the first sale of the day brings good luck (a concept called *penglaris*), making them more willing to offer significant discounts to early-bird shoppers.
