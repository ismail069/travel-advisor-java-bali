## English

Tenganan Pegringsingan Village is a Bali Aga community in Karangasem Regency known for its traditional settlement layout, preserved customs, woven crafts, and geringsing textiles produced using a double-ikat weaving technique.

## FAQ

### What makes Tenganan Pegringsingan distinctive?
The village is known for its Bali Aga traditions and rare geringsing double-ikat textiles.

### Can visitors enter private homes?
Only when invited or when an area is clearly open to visitors.

### Is photography allowed?
General photography may be permitted, but visitors should always ask before photographing residents, ceremonies, interiors, or textile-making processes.

### Can visitors buy authentic geringsing textiles?
Textiles and crafts may be available from local artisans. Verify provenance, production method, and pricing directly with the seller.
