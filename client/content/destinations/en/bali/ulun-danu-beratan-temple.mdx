## Overview — English

Ulun Danu Beratan Temple stands on the shore of Lake Beratan in Candikuning, within the Bedugul highlands. The complex is known for tiered meru shrines that appear integrated with the lake, especially when water level and light conditions are favorable.

Bedugul weather differs from Bali’s southern coast. Temperatures are cooler, cloud can build quickly, and rain is more common. Morning usually offers the best chance of clearer views and a more comfortable atmosphere before tour groups increase.

The complex remains a place of worship. Visitors should respect barriers, ceremonies, and areas not open to tourism. Current 2026 listings disagree on opening times and indicate recent price changes, so final admission and operating hours must be confirmed directly with the operator.
