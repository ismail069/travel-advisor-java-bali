## Overview — English

Banjar Hot Springs is a natural hot-spring bathing complex in North Bali. Its main pool is known for stone dragon-head spouts, while the site also provides rest areas and additional pool options.

The official website currently states daily opening hours of 08:30–17:30. The same site contains slightly inconsistent wording in its footer, so operating hours should still be confirmed before departure. Admission and additional costs such as lockers or private-pool services also require direct verification.

Visitors should follow swimwear, hygiene, and pool-safety rules. The mineral water may have a distinctive odor. People with heart conditions, blood-pressure concerns, pregnancy, or other medical conditions should seek professional advice before prolonged soaking.
