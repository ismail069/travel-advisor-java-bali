## Ikhtisar — Bahasa Indonesia

Banyumala Twin Waterfalls berada di kawasan Wanagiri, Bali utara, dan dikenal karena dua aliran utama yang jatuh ke kolam alami berwarna hijau. Untuk mencapainya, pengunjung harus menuruni jalur yang kemudian didaki kembali.

Tantangan utama bukan jarak semata, melainkan kombinasi tangga, kemiringan, permukaan basah, dan kelembapan. Wisatawan dengan masalah lutut atau keseimbangan perlu mempertimbangkan kemampuan kembali ke area parkir. Beberapa laporan menyebut adanya layanan ojek lokal untuk sebagian rute, tetapi ketersediaannya tidak boleh dianggap pasti.

Jam buka yang sering muncul adalah sekitar 08.00–18.00, namun sumber pihak ketiga tidak selalu konsisten. Harga tiket dan kondisi akses harus diperiksa di pintu masuk resmi.

<TravelTips title="Tips Kunjungan" items={["Wear shoes with strong grip.", "Bring dry clothing if swimming.", "Avoid descending during severe rain.", "Reserve energy for the uphill return."]} />

## Overview — English

Banyumala Twin Waterfalls is located in Wanagiri, North Bali, and is known for two main streams dropping into a green natural pool. Reaching it requires a descent that must later be climbed again.

The main challenge is not distance alone but the combination of steps, gradients, wet surfaces, and humidity. Travelers with knee or balance concerns should consider the return climb carefully. Some recent visitor reports mention local motorcycle assistance for part of the route, but availability should not be assumed.

Frequently listed hours are around 08:00–18:00, although third-party sources are not fully consistent. Admission and access conditions should be confirmed at the official entrance.
