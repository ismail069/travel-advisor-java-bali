## Ringkasan — Bahasa Indonesia

Pura Taman Saraswati, juga dikenal sebagai Ubud Water Palace, merupakan pura di pusat Ubud yang didedikasikan kepada Dewi Saraswati, dewi pengetahuan, sastra, dan seni. Daya tarik visual utamanya adalah jalur menuju pura yang diapit dua kolam teratai.

Lokasinya sangat strategis. Pura berada dekat Ubud Palace, Ubud Art Market, Museum Puri Lukisan, dan kawasan restoran Jalan Raya Ubud. Kunjungan singkat dapat dimasukkan ke rute jalan kaki pusat kota tanpa kendaraan tambahan.

Pura ini tetap merupakan tempat suci. Pengunjung harus mengikuti aturan pakaian, pembatas area, dan kegiatan persembahyangan yang sedang berlangsung. Jangan menganggap semua bagian kompleks terbuka untuk wisata.

## Overview — English

Pura Taman Saraswati, also known as the Ubud Water Palace, is a central Ubud temple dedicated to Dewi Saraswati, the Hindu goddess of knowledge, literature, and the arts. Its defining visual feature is the approach to the temple between two lotus ponds.

The location is highly convenient. The temple is close to Ubud Palace, Ubud Art Market, Museum Puri Lukisan, and the restaurants along Jalan Raya Ubud. A short visit fits naturally into a central-town walking route.

The temple remains a sacred place. Visitors must follow dress rules, respect restricted areas, and avoid disturbing worship. Not every section of the complex is open for tourism.

## Best Time

Morning provides softer light, lower heat, and fewer visitors. Late afternoon works well when combining the temple with dinner or an evening performance. Lotus flowering changes with season and plant condition.

## Temple Etiquette

- Dress modestly.
- Wear the required sarong or sash.
- Do not enter restricted sacred areas.
- Avoid blocking worshippers.
- Keep voices low.
- Ask permission before photographing people.
- Do not touch offerings.
