# Bilingual Guide

Indonesian and English content must preserve the same facts while sounding natural in each language.

## Bahasa Indonesia

- Clear and practical
- Suitable for domestic travelers
- Explain unfamiliar terms
- Avoid stiff translation language

## English

- Natural international travel style
- Explain local cultural terms
- Do not mirror Indonesian sentence structure
- Keep facts and recommendations consistent

Do not translate line by line.
