# Writing Standard

Every page must help a traveler answer:

- Is this destination worth visiting?
- Who is it suitable for?
- How much time is needed?
- What practical limitations should be expected?
- What should be combined with it?

## Rules

- Use original wording.
- Avoid unsupported superlatives.
- Explain trade-offs.
- Do not fabricate first-hand experience.
- Separate stable facts from changing information.
- Avoid padding content only to increase word count.
