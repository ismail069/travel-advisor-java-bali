## English
Tulamben is an East Bali coastal area renowned for diving and snorkeling, with an underwater shipwreck, volcanic-stone beaches, and convenient shore-entry dive sites.

## Safety
- Use a reputable dive operator.
- Confirm certification and medical requirements.
- Check currents, visibility, weather, and volcanic advisories.
- Wear suitable footwear for the rocky shoreline.
- Follow buddy-system and no-fly guidance.

## Accessibility
Shore entries often cross loose volcanic stones and uneven surfaces. Verify assisted entry and accessible accommodation directly with dive operators.
