## Bahasa Indonesia

Pura Besakih adalah kompleks pura terbesar dan paling penting di Bali, terletak di lereng Gunung Agung. Kawasan ini terdiri atas banyak pura, halaman bertingkat, gerbang monumental, dan jalur pejalan kaki dengan pemandangan pegunungan.

## English

Besakih Temple is Bali's largest and most important temple complex, located on the slopes of Mount Agung. The site includes numerous temples, tiered courtyards, monumental gateways, and walking routes with mountain views.

## FAQ

### Is a sarong required?
Temple dress is generally required. Verify whether the current ticket includes a sarong and sash.

### Can visitors enter every temple area?
No. Some areas may be restricted to worshippers or closed during ceremonies.

### Is a guide mandatory?
Verify the latest official visitor policy and ticket inclusions before arrival.

### Is the site suitable for children?
Yes with supervision, but expect stairs, long walks, and active religious areas.
