## English

Lempuyang Temple is a sacred temple complex in East Bali set across a hilly landscape. It is known for its split gateway facing Mount Agung and a series of temples located along an uphill pilgrimage route.

## FAQ

### Is the famous gateway the entire temple complex?
No. It is one section within a larger sacred temple complex.

### Is a sarong required?
Temple dress is generally required. Verify whether current admission includes a sarong and sash.

### Is the Mount Agung view guaranteed?
No. Visibility depends on weather, clouds, and seasonal conditions.

### Is the full pilgrimage route easy?
No. The complete route can involve many stairs and significant physical effort.
