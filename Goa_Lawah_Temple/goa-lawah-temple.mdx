## English

Goa Lawah Temple is an important coastal temple in Klungkung built around a cave inhabited by large numbers of bats. The complex has major spiritual significance and frequently hosts Balinese Hindu ceremonies.

## FAQ

### Why is Goa Lawah famous?
It is known for its sacred cave, large bat colony, and importance in Balinese Hindu religious traditions.

### Can visitors enter the cave?
Access is generally restricted. Visitors should remain within designated public areas and follow temple instructions.

### Is a sarong required?
Temple dress is generally required. Verify whether a sarong and sash are included with admission.

### Is photography allowed?
General photography may be permitted in public areas, but ceremonies, worshippers, and restricted spaces should not be photographed without permission.
