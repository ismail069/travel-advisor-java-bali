## Bahasa Indonesia

Padangbai adalah kota pelabuhan di Bali Timur yang menjadi titik keberangkatan kapal menuju Lombok dan Kepulauan Gili. Kawasan ini juga dikenal dengan pantai kecil, aktivitas snorkeling dan menyelam, pura pesisir, serta suasana desa nelayan.

## English

Padangbai is a port town in East Bali and a major departure point for ferries and fast boats to Lombok and the Gili Islands. The area is also known for small beaches, snorkeling and diving, coastal temples, and a fishing-village atmosphere.

## FAQ

No. It is also a destination for diving, snorkeling, beaches, food, and coastal culture.

Arrival requirements vary by operator and route. Confirm the latest check-in time directly with the provider.

### Is snorkeling suitable for beginners?
Some areas may suit beginners under calm conditions, but currents and visibility can change. Use a reputable operator.

### Can visitors walk between the harbor and nearby beaches?
Some locations are walkable, while others involve hills, steps, or local transport.
